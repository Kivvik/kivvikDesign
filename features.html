How Kivvik Works

A beautifully simple experience, designed to bolster client relationships.

A simple 3 step process

We send your clients a secure email link allowing them to submit an update in less 30 seconds. No need to login or download an app.

1. Client Import
2. Client Process
3. Reporting

Step 1

Add your clients

Getting started is extremely easy. Simply add your clients from your CRM by either using one of our single sign-on integration partners or uploading clients via a CSV file.

Once added, you can select how often you would like to check in with them. Either every 30, 60, or 90 days to customize individual outreach frequencies per client.

Step 2

Client completes survey

With our comprehensive list of life events, your client can easily select relevant life events about which to inform you.

Your client selects relevant update from our list of life events.

Your client then enters any additional information related to their event.

Your client then submits their update. The entire process usually takes 15-20 seconds!

Step 3

Integrated client reporting

Auto-triggered activities & notes

CRM integratons will allow advisors to auto-create tasks, schedule meetings, and assign notes to specific clients within their CRM once a client submits a new life update.

Email Reporting

Once a client submits you receive is an email notification with all the details. Within the email you can reply, call the client, or just analyze the information later on our dashboard.
