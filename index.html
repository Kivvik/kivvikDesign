College Savings

Vehicle (Buy / Sell)

See when your client intends to buy a new car, boat, or other vehicle, and help them factor it into their personal finances.

Travel Plans

Help your clients plan for any vacation or extended time away. Advise them on expenses, and make a couple recommendations to assist them!

Marriage / Engagement

An engagement leading to a wedding is one of the largest event in a person’s life, and most people do not plan for unforeseen related expenses that may impact their financial standing.

Real Estate

Real Estate tends to be one of the higher priced assets most people encounter. Help your clients navigate the complicated waters of the housing industry.

Renovations

Renovations can be planned for until unforeseen events cause costs to skyrocket. Help your clients create a financial safety net to help them realize their vision.

Retirement Changes

Retirement plans can change for all sorts of reasons, from an expansion of the family, to a change in a person’s career. Reach out to clients to help them find the most optimal approach for their retirements.

Employment Changes

For most people, jobs and careers change over time. Help your client understand changes in cash flow to ensure they are putting the most toward both their long-term goals and immediate needs.

Health Issues

Unexpected health issues can arise at any point in anyone’s life. Assist clients in plan for stressful periods by ensuring that they have all the resources they need to make it through financially intact.

Inheritance

Inheritances and gifts can come all in all forms of assets. Help clients understand how to grow these assets and plan for the tax implications to keep them protected.

Divorce / Separation

Separation and divorce are times when financial turmoil is imminent. Help your clients plan through an event that affects all aspects of their lives.
