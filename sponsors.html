Who we are
the team

BRIAN O. WRIGHT, CFP®

Brian O. Wright, CFP® is the co-founder of UniteWright, LLC, a fee-only financial planning firm that works primarily with primarily with young couples. He is also the co-founder of FA Bean Counters, a bookkeeping and accounting business for financial advisors.

Brian graduated from Indiana State University with a major in Business Administration and minor in Economics. He has an MBA from Anderson University.

Brian sits on the Board of Directors for the Financial Planning Association of Greater Indiana and is a Founding Member of the XY Planning Network. He is an advisory board member for the CFP® Board Certified Financial Planning major at Indiana State University.

Brian's LinkedIn Profile

KATE HOLMES, CFP®

Kate Holmes is the founder of Belmore Financial, LLC and Employee Financial Advice, which she launched in her quest to push the industry forward.

Kate graduated from RMIT University in Melbourne, Australia with a BA in Photography. She loves traveling and has been to almost 30 countries. She sees a growing need for financial planning internationally and is excited to take My Money Conference to other countries in the coming years.

Kate is active in the financial planning profession, serving on CFP Board’s Women’s Initiative Council, as President-Elect of FPA of Nevada, on other committees within FPA and NAPFA, and on the Board of Advisors for Kivvik, Inc., a financial technology startup.

She is often seen speaking at industry conferences around the U.S. and abroad, and is regularly quoted or featured in the media.
